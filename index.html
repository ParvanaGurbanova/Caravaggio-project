Introduction

We started with a SPARQL query to identify Cultural Properties in ArCo related to The Calling of Saint Matthew. The goal was to verify whether the Knowledge Graph contains explicit references to Caravaggio’s artwork.

We used FILTER and REGEX to search for different variations such as “vocazione di San Matteo”, “san matteo”, and “caravaggio”.

The results showed ambiguity because some entities refer to Caravaggio and others to similar names.
