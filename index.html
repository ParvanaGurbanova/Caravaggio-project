Introduction

We started with a SPARQL query to identify Cultural Properties in ArCo related to the iconography of The Calling of Saint Matthew. The goal was to verify whether the Knowledge Graph contains explicit references to the artwork associated with Caravaggio.

We used FILTER combined with REGEX to search for different textual variations such as “vocazione di San Matteo”, “san matteo”, and “caravaggio”. DISTINCT was used to remove duplicates.

The results showed heterogeneous cultural objects related to Saint Matthew, including paintings, sculptures, and decorative works. Some results refer to Caravaggio, while others refer to similar stylistic names.

After identifying candidate Cultural Properties, we refined the analysis by focusing on author attribution.
